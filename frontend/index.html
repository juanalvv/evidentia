How it works

From draft to evidence map in three steps.

Evidentia runs an autonomous academic review workflow so students and researchers can see where a paper is strong, stale, or missing support.

01

Upload or paste

Send a PDF draft or raw text. The system extracts claims, citations, and methods context.

02

Agents investigate

Nemotron-powered agents check Crossref, Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex, and arXiv for live evidence.

03

Review the report

Get source grades, coverage gaps, counterarguments, and supporting papers in one readable report.

What you get

A reviewer that shows its work.

The output is designed for quick decisions: what to replace, what to defend, and what evidence to add before submission.

Citation health

Per-source grades for recency, metadata quality, and superseded research risk.

Claim coverage

Coverage scoring that shows which claims are backed by citations and which need stronger evidence.

Counter-literature

Opposing papers grouped by claim, with summaries that make the disagreement easy to understand.

Methods context

Compares data and methods against field norms so weak methodology is visible early.
