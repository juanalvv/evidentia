Recent advances in transformer architectures have dramatically improved performance across NLP tasks. Vaswani et al. (2017) introduced the Transformer model — "Attention Is All You Need" (arXiv:1706.03762), removing recurrence in favor of attention. Devlin et al. (2018) showed strong downstream gains from pretraining in BERT (arXiv:1810.04805). In computer vision, deep residual networks produced major improvements (He et al., 2016; CVPR; DOI:10.1109/CVPR.2016.90; https://doi.org/10.1109/CVPR.2016.90\). These foundational works are often indexed with arXiv IDs or DOIs and should be picked up by Crossref/OpenAlex.
